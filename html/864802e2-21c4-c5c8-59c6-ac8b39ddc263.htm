Remarks

A ConvexPolyhedron is similar to a ConvexHullOfPoints except that the shape is fixed and the points that represent the shape cannot be changed. The ConvexPolyhedron is optimized and provides a faster support mapping (see GetSupportPoint(Vector3F)) than the ConvexHullOfPoints.

Use a ConvexHullOfPoints if the points in the shape need to be modified at runtime.
