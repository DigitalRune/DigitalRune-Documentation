Remarks
The InnerRadius and the OuterRadius define a spherical shell. Random values that are created are from the volume of this shell. If InnerRadius is 0 (default), random values from the whole sphere volume are created. If InnerRadius is equal to OuterRadius, all random positions lie on the surface of the sphere.
