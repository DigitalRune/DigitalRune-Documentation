Remarks
The swing limits form a deformed cone around the twist axis. This method can be used to get points to draw a debug visualization of the swing limit cone. Call this method for angles between 0 to 360°. Draw lines between neighbor points and the points and the cone tip. This creates a wire frame visualization of the swing limit cone.
