Remarks
Contact welding is a process that improves collision detection contacts at the edges of triangles. Additional information is stored with the shape to support welding. If the mesh is only used for Boolean queries or for ray casting, contact welding is not needed and EnableContactWelding can be set to . Further, contact welding is not supported for two-sided meshes (see IsTwoSided).
