Remarks

The SkyRenderer handles CloudLayerNodes, GradientSkyNodes, GradientTextureSkyNodes, ScatteringSkyNodes, SkyboxNodes, SkyObjectNodes, and StarfieldNodes.

Render Target and Viewport:
This renderer renders into the current render target and viewport of the graphics device.
