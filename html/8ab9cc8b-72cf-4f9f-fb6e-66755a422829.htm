Type: SrtTransform
The product of srt1 and srt2: srt1 * srt2.
Remarks

When product (srt1 * srt2) is applied to a vector v the transformation are applied in the following order: v' = srt1 * srt2 * v
That means, the vector is first transformed by srt2 and then by srt1.
