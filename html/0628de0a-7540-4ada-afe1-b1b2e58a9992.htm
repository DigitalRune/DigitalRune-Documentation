Multithreading and thread-safety

General notes on thread-safety in the DigitalRune Engine

  • While an update of a "service" is in progress (e.g. CollisionDomainUpdate, SimulationUpdate, GraphicsManagerUpdate, etc.), the service state must not be read or modified. For example:
    • While CollisionDomainUpdate is running, do not use CollisionDomainGetContacts.
    • While SimulationUpdate is running, do not add new rigid bodies to SimulationRigidBodies.
    • Etc.
  • Adding/removing objects to/from collections (e.g. CollisionDomainCollisionObjects, SimulationRigidBodies, IGraphicsServiceScreens, etc.) is not thread-safe and must be synchronized manually.

When in doubt about thread-safety, ask in our Forum. As we learn more about how the DigitalRune users use the API, we will try to improve our documentation.

Thread-safety in DigitalRune Geometry

  • Querying ISpatialPartitionTs in parallel is safe (e.g. AabbTreeTGetOverlaps).
  • Calling CollisionAlgorithms in parallel is safe (e.g. GetContacts).
