Remarks

A LensFlareNode position a effect in a 3D scene. Lens flares can be caused by local lights or directional lights, such as the sun. If the lens flare effect is caused by a directional light then the light direction is defined by the local forward direction (0, 0, -1) of the scene node.

Cloning: When a LensFlareNode is cloned the LensFlare is not duplicated. The LensFlare is copied by reference (shallow copy). The original LensFlareNode and the cloned instance will reference the same LensFlare object.
