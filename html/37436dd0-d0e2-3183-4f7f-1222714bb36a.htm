Remarks
DoT(IEnumerableT, ActionT) is similar to ForEachT(IEnumerableT, ActionT). The difference is that ForEachT(IEnumerableT, ActionT) immediately enumerates the given sequence. DoT(IEnumerableT, ActionT) does not automatically trigger the enumeration. Instead it returns the IEnumerableT.
